AIDEN'S REVIEW PLAYLIST

10. Whack Your __

A flash series where you as the player click on items in the room to whack whatever the title is. (Ex, boss, computer etc.)

9. Line Rider

A sandbox game where you create lines for the man on the sled to ride on. Most famously on the internet is people creating music syncs.

8. VVVVVV

A puzzle platform game where the goal is to return your spaceship crew back home. You can flip gravity at the push of a button

7. Blozorz

A puzzle game all about getting the rectangle in the correct orientation to move across the map to fit in the square hole

6. Plants vs. Zombies

A tower defense style game where you collect suns to plant plats that will defend against waves of zombies

5. Super Smash Flash 2

Basically Nintendo’s Super Smash Bros Melee as a flash game but with more characters such as Goku and Naruto

4. Skywire 2

A game where you transport 3 people across a skywire and avoid obstacles to keep the people in the carriage.

3. Raft Wars

A turn based puzzle game where the goal was to knock the opposing pirates off their ships

2. FireBoy and WaterGirl

A puzzle platforming game where the gimick is that you control one character with “wasd” and the other character with the arrow keys.

1. Club Penguin

MMO Game where you are able to live your life as a penguin, raising pets called puffles and owning your own igloo.
